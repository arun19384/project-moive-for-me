Features

Everything your watch habit deserves

One private shelf for movies, series and anime — with discovery, stats and collectibles built in.

My Shelf

Your whole watched library in one grid. Search, filter by type or year, and sort by rating, date or A–Z.

Trending

See what's hot this week from TMDB across movies, series and anime — add any title to your shelf in one tap.

Quick Add

Search IMDb to auto-fill the poster, title and year — then set your rating, the date, platform and notes.

Watchlist

Build a want-to-watch queue. Can't decide? Spin “What should I watch tonight?” and let the app pick.

Stats & Insights

Milestones, streaks, a 90-day heatmap, your “watch DNA” vibe, a lifetime Top 10 and rating breakdowns.

Collections

Custom shelves, franchise sagas to complete, and collectible badges across four rarity tiers.

Profile & Sync

Use it instantly as a guest, or create an account to sync your shelf and collections across devices.

Dark & Light

An elegant dark gold theme by default, with a one-tap light mode — your choice is remembered.

How to use

From first title to full shelf in minutes

Five simple steps — no setup, no clutter.

1

Search & add a title

Tap the gold + button, search IMDb, and pick your film, series or anime — poster and details auto-fill.

2

Rate it & log the details

Give it a score from 1–10, set the date you watched it, the platform, and any notes you want to keep.

3

Build your watchlist

Save things for later, then spin “What should I watch tonight?” and check where it's streaming.

4

Watch your stats grow

Head to Stats to track milestones, streaks, your heatmap, and your lifetime Top 10.

5

Earn badges & complete sagas

Finish franchises and hit milestones to unlock collectible badges — then show one off next to your name.

Install it like a real app

Do Young is a PWA — add it to your home screen and it opens full-screen, offline-ready, with its own icon. No app store needed.

Open the app

Open this page on your phone, then follow the steps to add it to your home screen.

1Open this site in Safari on your iPhone or iPad.
2Tap the Share button in the toolbar.
3Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
4Tap Add — Do Young now lives on your home screen. 🎉
1Open this site in Chrome on your Android phone.
